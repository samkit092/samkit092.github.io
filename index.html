#ColourMe

eCommerce - Commercial, Watsons International

#ColourMe was a virtual makeover tool embedded within our flagship Watsons App. It offered customers the chance to try on different makeup and hair colour products at just their fingertips. The tool was proven very useful in driving customer excitement especially at times when customers are less likely to visit our brick-and-mortar shops due to global pandemic situation. #ColourMe users were found to be 50% more likely to convert compared with general users of the App. They also had a stronger engagement.

Foundation Finder

eCommerce - Commercial, Watsons International

Foundation Finder was another digital tool embedded within our flagship Watsons App. It resolves customer pain points by allowing them to find their perfect foundation shade. Customers can simply take a selfie and answer a few simple questions about their skin conditions and makeup style preferences, and the app will recommend foundations that best suit the customers' needs. It also gives the option for customers to then choose a lighter or darker shade based on their individual preference. The function is digitally connected to the Watsons online store, so customers can immediately order their recommended products and have it delivered to their homes or pick up in stores. They can also save the recommendation via email for later reference when visiting the stores. The tool has been widely covered in the media.
